# AlorA Image Generation Prompts

## Brand Visual Guidelines
- **Lighting**: Warm, golden hour natural light; soft studio lighting with intentional shadows
- **Photography Mix**: 60% lifestyle/editorial, 40% clean studio product shots
- **Details**: Subtle line drawings (jasmine buds, lotus, elephant motifs) woven into backgrounds or fabrics
- **Aesthetic**: Handmade luxury, artisanal Indian heritage meets contemporary minimalism
- **Color Palette**: Warm neutrals, jewel tones, ivory, gold accents
- **Mood**: Intimate, intentional, lived-in, warm, not sterile

---

## HERO IMAGES (Seasonal Rotation)

### Summer / MOKSH - "Woven for movement"
**Prompt:**
"A flowing teal linen dress photographed in warm golden-hour light outdoors. Woman mid-movement, fabric in motion, sun-lit and ethereal. Subtle line drawing of jasmine buds embroidered along the hem. Warm, natural, artisanal. Shot on film. Soft focus background with trees. Luxury but handmade feeling."

**Alternative:**
"Teal dress draped on a wooden stand in natural light. Hands adjusting fabric, showing care and intention. Warmth and texture emphasized. Minimalist studio setting with pale background."

---

### Monsoon / ANUBHAV - "Felt in every stitch"
**Prompt:**
"Deep emerald or sapphire dress photographed in warm studio light, close enough to see fabric texture and hand-stitched details. Artisan hands visible, touching the fabric gently. Subtle gold thread or lotus line drawing detail in the weave. Handmade luxury. Shot like documentary photography of craft."

**Alternative:**
"Rich jewel-tone dress on a form, shot in soft warm light. Detail shots of seams, stitching, embroidery. Close-up showing heritage textile techniques. Intimate and careful."

---

### Winter / TAARA - "Worn close, worn bright"
**Prompt:**
"Gold or luminous cream dress on dark background (midnight blue or charcoal). Studio lighting sculpts the fabric, creating dramatic shadows. Warm, intimate feel. Subtle line drawing of elephant motif or lotus pattern visible in the weave. Luxury, reserved, glowing. Fine art photography style."

**Alternative:**
"Pale luminous dress photographed against black velvet. Soft side-lighting creates depth and dimension. Studio setting. High-end fashion editorial style. Warm undertones throughout."

---

### Spring / NIYYAT - "Made with intention"
**Prompt:**
"Soft blush, pale sage, or cream dress photographed in warm natural morning light. Fresh flowers (jasmine, marigold, or lotus blossoms) around or held by hands visible in frame. Lifestyle editorial style. Hands show care and intention in wearing. Artisanal, heritage-inspired, romantic but modern."

**Alternative:**
"Pale dress in a garden or natural setting with botanical elements. Warmth and softness emphasized. Shot like a slow-fashion editorial. Handcrafted feel visible in details."

## CRAFT SECTION IMAGES

### Artisans
**Prompt:**
"Close-up of skilled hands working on fabric — cutting, stitching, or embroidering a dress. Warm studio light. Shows decades of expertise and care. Focus on the hands and the craft. Textile artisan at work. Documentary photography style. Shows intention and heritage knowledge in the gesture."

---

### Fabrics
**Prompt:**
"Luxurious natural fabrics draped beautifully — silks, linens, cottons — in warm light. Show texture, weave, and how cloth moves. Subtle line drawings of lotus or floral motifs visible in some fabrics. Artisanal weaver's workshop aesthetic. Close-up detail shots showing quality and craftsmanship."

---

### Process
**Prompt:**
"The making process: cutting, dyeing, stitching, finishing. Multiple scenes in warm natural and studio light. Show hands at work, the care taken, no rushing. Artisan-focused. Documentary photography of luxury handmade process. Heritage craft techniques visible."

## PRODUCT GRID IMAGES

### General Product Shot Guidelines
"Studio lighting, warm background (ivory or soft gray). Dress on form or lay-flat. Show: fabric quality, drape, any embroidery detail, color depth. Artisanal but refined. Clean, not sterile. Subtle line drawings of jasmine, lotus, or elephant motifs visible in fabric or embroidery. Shot like editorial luxury fashion but with handmade feeling."

### By Collection:

**NIYYAT (Intention):** Soft, purposeful, grounded. Minimal backgrounds. Fabric detail emphasized.

**ANUBHAV (Experience):** Textured, rich, sensory. Close-ups showing fabric movement and craft details.

**TAARA (Star):** Luminous, dramatic, elevated. Studio lighting sculpts the garment beautifully.

**MOKSH (Freedom):** Movement, ease, fluidity. Dress in motion or draped for movement. Light and airy.

## IMPLEMENTATION

Use these prompts with:
- **Midjourney** (v6+): Add "--ar 3:4" or "--ar 4:5" for dress proportions
- **DALL-E 3**: Add "professional photography, editorial style, warm lighting"
- **Adobe Firefly** or **Stable Diffusion**: Use as-is

**Key phrase to add across all prompts:**
"Artisanal Indian luxury, heritage meets contemporary, warm natural lighting, handmade quality visible, editorial photography"

---

## Notes for Image Generation
- Keep Indian/heritage elements subtle (embroidery, line drawings, patterns)
- Warm color grading across all images
- Natural light prioritized over studio when possible
- Handmade quality visible but refined
- No cheap or generic stock-photo feeling
- Each image tells a story of craft and intention
